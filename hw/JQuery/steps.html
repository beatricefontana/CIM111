Step-By-Step Guide

Show

  1. Assemble all elements together.
  2. Pour the nut butter into all the ingredients.
  3. Start mixing them all, exept the coconut flakes. It should resemble a somewhat sticky dough that holds together when lightly squeezed.
  4. Shape them into a spherical shape with your hands.
  5. Pour the coconut flakes over them and enjoy!
