Energy Bites

Ingredients

  • Oats. This is one is my favorite cereal to use as a flour because it very clean, filling and easy to manage.
  • Nut butter. Nut butters, like peanut butter or almond butter, help stick all the ingredients together and adds that extra yum that we all deserve.
  • Coconout flakes. To make it look like they are little snowballs and give it a lil more sweetness.
  • Mix-ins! Here you can be as creative and fancy as you like- superfood additions, such as flaxseeds and chia seeds, can boost your immune system and other ingredients like chocolate (which I personally do not adore) can make it perfect to your taste.

  • Assemble all elements together.


  • Pour the nut butter into all the ingredients.

ul>
  • Start mixing them all, exept the coconut flakes. It should resemble a somewhat sticky dough that holds together when lightly squeezed.

  • Shape them into a spherical shape with your hands.


  • Pour the coconut flakes over them and enjoy!
